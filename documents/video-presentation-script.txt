Video presentation
------------------

Hi, my name is Alex and I will show you what Diagramo is.

Diagramo is an online diagram editor. It was built using ONLY 
HTML5 - no Flash or other plugins.

Draw
-----
Let's do a simple diagram
Take a rounded rectangle, drop it 
Take another one, drop it
Draw a line between them.

Change their color.


Share
------
You can share it with anyone.

Go to Collaborators section and invite people.


Export
-------
You can export it as SVG, PNG or JPG.

Action
------
Make an account or download it for your server.
